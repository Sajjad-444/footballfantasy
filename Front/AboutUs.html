What is football fantasy?

Fantasy football is a game that casts you in the role of a Fantasy Manager, given the task to pick a squad of real-life players who score points for your team based on their real-life performances in their matches.

To play Fantasy Football you’ll need to pick a team of 11 or sometimes a squad of 15 real-life players or more,that will represent you each week of the season. Over each of those weeks they will score you points which will add and subtract to a total that will be compared to other teams in your mini league and/or in the Fantasy Game world overall with prizes at stake.

How do I pick my team or Squad?

Every Fantasy Football game will hand you a budget which you spend on your team or squad. If you new to Fantasy Football, this budget may well seem generous but once you take a look at the player list and start selecting, it’s a good bet that it will start getting eaten up pretty quickly.

How to have a strong team?

In fantasy football, by having a team that you have made, you can play with other teams made by others and achieve the experience of a coach by leading your team.
You can have great players like Halaand, Mbappe, Messi, Ronaldo and ... in your team, but having great players definitely requires a lot of money. So, to access these great players, you have to win the match so that your score and budget will increase so that you can have these players in your team and your team will become stronger.
We hope you win all your competitions so that you can score a lot of points and achieve bigger goals in the game.

And finally, we hope you enjoy Football Fantasy.
